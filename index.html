From: Ashish Sood - General Partners, Curious Ventures
Subject: Owning the Distribution Moat

[ Executive Summary ]

"In an era where AI has commoditized code and Web3 has commoditized capital, Distribution is the only moat."

Curious Ventures is launching a $10 Million Pre-Seed Hybrid Fund built by leveraging an Archipelago of high-trust distribution nodes. We believe the internet is undergoing a massive Behavioral Fork where AI is transforming how users command software, and Web3 is revolutionizing asset ownership and retention.

Unlike traditional funds, we utilize a Hybrid Structure ($8M Equity / $2M Liquid) to balance long-term venture returns with early liquidity participation. By leveraging an "Archipelago" of distributed communities, we secure a distribution-first advantage for our founders.

[ 01 / Market Thesis ]

The Behavioral Fork

The AI Shift

Search → Commanding

Users no longer want to navigate menus, they expect software to act. We are moving from Chatbots to Large Action Models that execute complex tasks. The Agentic AI market is projected to grow from $5.25B in 2024 to ~$200B in 2034.

Web3 Maturity

Invisible Rails

After a decade of infrastructure building, Web3 is ready for the passenger experience. Winning protocols make the blockchain invisible through chain abstraction and invisible wallets that require no seed phrases, enabling mass consumer adoption.

[ 02 / Strategy ]

Hybrid & Disciplined

We deploy capital over a 3-year period to mitigate vintage risk, targeting ~15 core startup deals.

02 / Why It Works

THE SOVEREIGN
ADVANTAGE.

Legacy institutions and centralized platforms have become "high-noise, low-trust." As Balaji Srinivasan notes in The Network State, the future belongs to communities that can organize digitally and act physically.

The Archipelago works because it prioritizes Social Proximity over Physical Proximity. We bypass the gatekeepers by building a Sovereign Distribution Network.

The Logic

Decoupling from the Ad-Stack

Search and Social ads are a tax on innovation. High-trust nodes reduce Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) to near zero through peer-to-peer advocacy within the network.

The Importance
